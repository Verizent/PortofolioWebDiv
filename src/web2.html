WHAT IS NEXTSPACE?

Nextspace is a sort of child-company to the Next corporation. We are based in Indonesia and our main goal is to create a faster web browser for the community. Not only that, we want our community to feel secure and assured that their data are safe and not being given to another party without their consent. Our company currently have bases all around Indonesia and our main headquarter are situated in Jakarta. We are planning to expand our company to the international level.
